Latest news

The planet Mars will be at its closest possible distance from Earth in the night from Wednesday to Thursday. The red planet is therefore clearly visible. Extra special is that it is also a full moon. From Earth you can see that Mars briefly disappears behind the moon. To observe it from the Netherlands, you need a bit of luck and binoculars or a telescope.
